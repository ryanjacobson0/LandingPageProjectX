Features

What We Offer

Low Interest Rates

Our proprietary design shifts the risk away from the employee and towards the company allowing for safe investments and low commensurate interest rates.

Autonomus & Open Source

Smart contracts autonously execute the code thus no third party intervention is required. The open-source code allows anyone to verify how it functions.

Instant Aproval

No lengthy approval processes are required. Once we have verified the employees credit burden and employment contract, loans can be granted instantly!

The Marketplace

Increasing flexibility and decreasing risk

The investor receives payment directly from the company when the company pays salaries. Thereby shifting counter party risk from the employee to the company.

The investor only needs to be concerned about the risk that a company won't pay salaries and not about the risk of an employee not paying her loan.

How does it work

Submit an Orders

  • Employees & Investors can upload orders to the marketplace
  • They will stipulate the amount of money and the interest rate they are willing to pay/receive
  • The orders will be ranked and added to the marketplace

Get Matched

  • Investors & Employees can browse through the orders
  • They can accept an order that meets their requirements both in value and interest rate
  • The funds will transfer automatically once the order is accepted

Settle Up

  • Funds will flow from the company to the investor when salaries are paid
  • The employee will receive payment of her salary less the amount paid to the investor
  • The investor will receive the value stipulated less transaction fees
